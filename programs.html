Early Childhood Development (ECD)

Nurturing the potential of our youngest learners through engaging, play-based education.

Play-Based Learning

Our curriculum focuses on learning through play, helping children develop social, cognitive, and emotional skills naturally.

Safe & Stimulating Environment

We partner with schools that provide a secure and stimulating setting where young children can explore and grow with confidence.

Foundational Skills

Teachers guide students in developing foundational literacy and numeracy skills, preparing them for primary education.

Intensive English Program (IEP)

Accelerating English proficiency with a focused and immersive curriculum.

Focused Learning

IEP is designed for students who require a rapid improvement in their English skills. Our teachers focus on the four core skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing, using dynamic and interactive methods to maximize student engagement and retention.

English & International Programs (EP/IP)

Teaching core subjects in English to prepare students for a globalized world.

Beyond Language

In EP and IP settings, our teachers are not just language instructors; they are subject matter experts who teach subjects like Math, Science, and Social Studies in English. This approach enhances language fluency while building a strong academic foundation.

MediaKids English Camps

Making learning English a fun and unforgettable adventure!

Themed Activities

Each camp has an exciting theme, from "Space Adventure" to "Jungle Safari," that makes learning engaging and context-rich.

Project-Based Learning

Students work in teams on creative projects, developing communication, collaboration, and critical thinking skills.

Build Confidence

Our camps provide a supportive environment where students feel confident to practice English and make new friends.

MediaKids Awards

Celebrating Excellence in Education.

The MediaKids Awards is an annual event to recognize the outstanding contributions of our teachers and partner schools. We celebrate dedication, innovation, and the positive impact made in classrooms across Thailand.

Award Categories

  • Teacher of the Year
  • Rookie of the Year
  • Most Innovative Teacher
  • School Partner of the Year
  • Outstanding Community Engagement

Join the Celebration

The awards ceremony is a night of celebration, networking, and inspiration. Stay tuned for details on our next event!

Summer Camp in Canada

An immersive English language and cultural experience in beautiful Canada.

Learn and Explore

Our summer camp program offers students a unique opportunity to improve their English skills while exploring the stunning landscapes and vibrant cities of Canada. The program combines classroom learning with exciting excursions and activities.
