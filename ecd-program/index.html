What is ECD Program?

The program focuses on helping learners use English naturally in daily situations while building vocabulary, pronunciation, and confidence.

Our ECD curriculum is carefully aligned with Thai MOE standards and adapted for mixed-ability classrooms, ensuring that every student—beginner to advanced—can grow at their own pace.
