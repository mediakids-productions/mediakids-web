MediaKids Awards

The MediaKids Awards is a fun and motivating English competition designed to encourage students to develop their vocabulary, confidence, and communication skills. Held across partner schools nationwide, the competition offers an accessible and enjoyable way for students to challenge themselves.

With clear guidelines, fair scoring, and engaging activities, the MediaKids Awards gives every student—from beginner to advanced—a chance to shine. It's more than a competition; it's an opportunity to build confidence, teamwork, and a love for learning.
