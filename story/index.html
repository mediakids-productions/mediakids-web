Our Story

A Journey of Teaching, Culture, and Community

MediaKids Academy was founded in 2001 with a simple yet powerful goal: to support English education in Thailand through meaningful cultural exchange. What began as a small team dedicated to helping Thai schools quickly grew into a nationwide organization trusted by educators, families, and communities.

For more than two decades, we have expanded our programs, developed innovative learning materials, and partnered with over 70 schools across Thailand. Our journey has always been guided by the belief that every student deserves a learning experience that inspires curiosity, confidence, and connection to the world.

Throughout our journey, our core values have remained the same:

  • Putting students first through fun, practical, and meaningful learning experiences
  • Building strong teacher communities through support, training, and ongoing development
  • Partnering with schools to create sustainable, high-quality English programs
  • Celebrating culture, both Thai and international, as an essential part of education

More than just an education provider, MediaKids Academy is a bridge—connecting people, cultures, and ideas. Our history is a story of growth, collaboration, and the belief that every child deserves a chance to explore the world through language.

Our story continues—with the same commitment to connection, creativity, and community that started it all.
