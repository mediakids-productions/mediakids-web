The Importance of AI in Teaching and Learning Today

Learn why AI is becoming important in modern teaching and learning, and how teachers can use it responsibly to support students.

23 June 2026 · 3 min read · MediaKids Academy

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is becoming an important part of education around the world. From lesson planning to language learning, AI tools are changing the way teachers prepare lessons and the way students learn.

AI is not here to replace teachers. Instead, it can support teachers, save time, and create more engaging learning experiences for students.

What Is AI in Education?

AI in education refers to technology that can help teachers and students with learning tasks. This may include tools that suggest lesson ideas, create quizzes, check writing, translate language, or give students extra practice.

For English teachers, AI can be especially useful. It can help create vocabulary exercises, reading passages, dialogues, speaking prompts, and worksheets for different levels.

Why AI Is Important for Teachers

Teachers spend a lot of time planning lessons, preparing materials, and thinking of new classroom activities. AI can help reduce some of this workload.

For example, teachers can use AI to create short reading passages, grammar examples, warm-up questions, or review activities. This gives teachers more time to focus on students and classroom interaction.

Why AI Is Useful for Students

Students can use AI-supported tools to practice pronunciation, grammar, vocabulary, and writing. For shy students, AI can provide a low-pressure way to practice before speaking in front of classmates.

AI can also help students learn at their own pace.

Teachers Are Still the Most Important Part

Even though AI is powerful, teachers remain essential. AI cannot replace a teacher's care, encouragement, classroom energy, or personal connection with students.

AI can give suggestions, but teachers decide what is appropriate for their students.

The Bottom Line

AI is becoming an important part of modern education. The best classroom is not one where AI replaces teachers, but one where teachers use AI wisely to make learning more effective, creative, and meaningful.

For teachers in Thailand and around the world, learning how to use AI responsibly is now an important skill for the future of education.
