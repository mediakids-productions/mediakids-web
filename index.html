Early Childhood Development (ECD)

Nurturing the potential of our youngest learners through engaging, play-based education.

Play-Based Learning

Our curriculum focuses on learning through play, helping children develop social, cognitive, and emotional skills naturally.

Safe & Stimulating Environment

We partner with schools that provide a secure and stimulating setting where young children can explore and grow with confidence.

Foundational Skills

Teachers guide students in developing foundational literacy and numeracy skills, preparing them for primary education.

Intensive English Program (IEP)

Accelerating English proficiency with a focused and immersive curriculum.

Focused Learning

IEP is designed for students who require a rapid improvement in their English skills. Our teachers focus on the four core skills: listening, speaking, reading, and writing, using dynamic and interactive methods to maximize student engagement and retention.

English & International Programs (EP/IP)

Teaching core subjects in English to prepare students for a globalized world.

Beyond Language

In EP and IP settings, our teachers are not just language instructors; they are subject matter experts who teach subjects like Math, Science, and Social Studies in English. This approach enhances language fluency while building a strong academic foundation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common inquiries we receive.

You must have a Bachelor's degree (in any field) to obtain a work permit in Thailand. You should also be a native English speaker or have a high level of proficiency (e.g., TOEIC score of 800+). A TEFL/TESOL certificate is highly recommended and often required by schools.

While previous teaching experience is a plus, it's not always a requirement. We provide comprehensive pre-service training and ongoing support to equip new teachers with the skills needed for a successful semester. A positive attitude and a passion for working with children are most important!

Salaries are competitive and depend on your qualifications, experience, and the location of the school. A typical salary is more than enough to live a comfortable lifestyle in Thailand, allowing you to cover living expenses, travel, and save money.

Absolutely. This is one of our key services. We provide full, step-by-step guidance and support with all the necessary paperwork to obtain your Non-Immigrant B (Business) visa and your Thai work permit.

Our support is comprehensive. It starts with pre-departure guidance, an airport pickup, and a detailed orientation week. Once you are at your school, you will have a dedicated Thai consultant for any issues that may arise, from finding housing and opening a bank account to cultural or classroom-related questions.
