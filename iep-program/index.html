What is IEP Program?

IEP — Intensive English Program is designed for schools aiming to elevate English proficiency, prepare students for higher-level study, and create a strong academic English foundation.

With a textbook-supported curriculum and clear skill benchmarks, students receive systematic lessons with regular assessments to track their progress throughout the program.
